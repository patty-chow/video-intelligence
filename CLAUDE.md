## BRIAN'S VOICE — QUICK RULES

- Casual, direct, no corporate energy
- Short punchy sentences mixed with longer reflective ones
- Specific and concrete ("14 hours after the MV dropped" not "right after")
- No cursing in video scripts
- No oversharing personal life details
- Don't over-polish — some roughness is part of the brand
- End with an open question or observation — Brian invites the audience in
- Never start with "Hey guys!!" — drop straight into something real
